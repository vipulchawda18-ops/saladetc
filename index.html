Our Story

Born from a love
of real food

Salad ETC was born from a deeply personal realisation — that the food we eat every single day quietly shapes how we feel, how we heal, and how we live. Our inspiration came from the simple, transformative act of eating clean. Not dieting. Not starving. Just choosing light, nourishing, thoughtfully prepared food that your body actually recognises and thanks you for.

We started crafting lightly sautéed salads designed to deliver complete daily nutrition — meals rich in plant-based proteins, wholesome legumes, seasonal greens, and gut-friendly ingredients that work in harmony with your digestive system. Every bowl is built to support your body's natural detoxification process, reduce inflammation, and keep your energy steady through the day — without ever feeling heavy or dull.

What truly sets Salad ETC apart is that our menu isn't guesswork. Every recipe has been carefully designed in consultation with certified diet specialists, with a sharp focus on balanced macros, fibre, and micronutrients — so you're not just eating a salad, you're fuelling a healthier lifestyle. We believe that eating well should feel effortless, joyful, and absolutely delicious.
