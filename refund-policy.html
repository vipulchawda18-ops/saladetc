Refund & Cancellation Policy

Please read this carefully before placing an order or subscription.

At Salad etc. we strive to deliver fresh, hygienic, and high-quality food products. Due to the perishable nature of our products, the following refund and cancellation terms apply.
1

No Return Policy

All food products once delivered are non-returnable due to hygiene and safety reasons.

2

Order Cancellation

  • Orders once confirmed may not be cancelled if preparation has already started.
  • Cancellation requests must be raised before the scheduled preparation time.
3

Subscription Cancellation

Before First Delivery

Customers may cancel their subscription before the first scheduled delivery and receive a full refund. Refunds will be processed within 5–7 working days to the original payment method.

After Subscription Has Started

If a subscription is cancelled after deliveries have begun:

  • Charges will be calculated based on deliveries already used.
  • Standard rate applied: ₹150 per bowl/delivery.
  • The remaining balance will be refunded.

Refunds may be provided:

  • To the original payment source
4

Subscription Pause / Reschedule

Customers may request to pause or reschedule deliveries:

  • Inform at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Requests can be made via email or phone.
  • Missed deliveries without prior notice may not be compensated.
5

Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered only in the following cases:

  • Order not delivered
  • Wrong item delivered
  • Product damaged during delivery
  • Quality issue verified by our team

Salad etc. reserves the right to verify claims before processing refunds.

6

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided for:

  • Change of taste preference
  • Delivery delays caused by external factors
  • Incorrect address provided by customer
  • Failure to receive order at delivery time
7

Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 working days. Bank/payment gateway timelines may vary.
